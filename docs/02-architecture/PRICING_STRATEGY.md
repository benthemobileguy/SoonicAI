## 💰 Recommended Pricing Model: **Hybrid (Credits + Subscription)**

### Executive Summary

After analyzing your target market (worship pianists), usage patterns (irregular, 2-4 songs/week), and competitor pricing (screenshots analyzed), I recommend a **hybrid credits + subscription model**.

**Why this works:**
- ✅ Lower commitment barrier (credits vs monthly billing)
- ✅ Higher revenue per user ($24.90 one-time > $12/month recurring)
- ✅ Matches sporadic usage patterns
- ✅ Reduces churn (no monthly cancellations for credit buyers)
- ✅ Proven successful (per screenshot examples)

## 📊 Complete Pricing Structure

### **Tier 1: Free (Lead Magnet)** 🎁

```
Price: $0
Includes: 3 analyses
Reset: Never (lifetime limit)
Target: 100% of new users
```

**Purpose:**
- Let users experience the "wow moment"
- Validate value before paying
- Generate word-of-mouth marketing

**Why 3 analyses:**
- Enough to try different songs
- Creates urgency (scarcity drives conversion)
- Prevents abuse
- Industry standard for freemium

**Conversion Goal:** 10% of free users → paid

**Key Features:**
- ✅ **Credits never expire** (reduces purchase anxiety)
- ✅ **1 credit = 1 analysis** (simple, no confusion)
- ✅ **Bonus credits on larger packs** (incentivizes upsell)
- ✅ **No recurring billing** (psychological safety)

**Why Credits Work Best:**

1. **Matches Usage Pattern**
   - Worship pianists analyze 2-4 songs/week
   - Not daily usage (unlike email/CRM tools)
   - Sporadic need = credits > subscription

2. **Lower Commitment Barrier**
   - $24.90 one-time feels safer than $19.90/month
   - No fear of "forgetting to cancel"
   - More control over spending

3. **Higher ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)**
   - Standard Pack: $24.90 one-time
   - vs Monthly Sub: $19.90/month (user might cancel after Month 1)
   - Credits generate more upfront revenue

4. **Reduced Churn**
   - No monthly cancellations
   - Users retain credits for months
   - Natural upsell when credits run low

**Conversion Goal:** 70% of paid users buy credits

### **Tier 3: Monthly Subscription** 📅

```
Price: $19.90/month
Includes: Unlimited analyses
Best for: Music teachers, choir directors, professionals
Bonus: Early access to new features, priority support
```

**Why Monthly:**
- ✅ Captures power users (teachers, professionals)
- ✅ Predictable MRR (important for sustainability)
- ✅ Lower barrier than yearly commitment
- ✅ Affordable for worship market ($19.90 < $25-35 industry avg)

**Why $19.90 (not $12/month as originally planned):**
- Industry average: $25-35/month (your price is below)
- Anchored against yearly ($149/year = $12.42/month effective)
- Makes yearly look like better deal
- More sustainable revenue

**Conversion Goal:** 20% of paid users subscribe monthly

---

### **Tier 4: Yearly Subscription** 📆

```
Price: $149/year ($12.42/month effective)
Includes: Unlimited analyses
Save: $90 vs monthly (37% discount)
Bonus: Priority support, beta features, annual renewal discount
```

**Why Yearly:**
- ✅ **Upfront cash** (helps runway significantly)
- ✅ **Reduces churn** (annual commitment)
- ✅ **Higher LTV** (lifetime value)
- ✅ **Psychological anchor** (makes monthly seem cheap)

**Conversion Goal:** 10% of paid users subscribe yearly

---

### **Tier 5: Lifetime (Limited Launch Special)** ♾️

```
Price: $399 (Regular: $599)
Availability: First 50 customers only
Includes: Unlimited analyses forever, all future features
Bonus: Founding member badge, priority support forever
```

**⚠️ Important Constraints:**

1. **Launch Special ONLY**
   - Offer for first 30-60 days
   - Cap at 50-100 customers
   - Remove after Month 2

2. **Why Offer Lifetime?**
   - ✅ Generates immediate cash for runway
   - ✅ Creates evangelists (lifetime users = loyal advocates)
   - ✅ Creates FOMO (urgency drives conversions)
   - ✅ Social proof (show "37/50 sold" progress bar)

3. **Why Remove After Launch?**
   - ❌ Kills long-term recurring revenue
   - ❌ Limits future revenue per user
   - ❌ Unsustainable at scale

**Conversion Goal:** 5-10 lifetime buyers (generates $2K-4K upfront)

**After Month 2:** Remove lifetime tier entirely

## 🛠️ Implementation Checklist

### Database Schema (Already Done ✅)

Your Supabase schema supports:

```sql
profiles table:
- plan: 'free' | 'credits' | 'pro'
- analyses_limit: INT (3 for free, NULL for pro)
- credits_remaining: INT (for credit purchases)
- subscription_status: 'active' | 'canceled' | 'past_due'

credit_purchases table:
- amount: DECIMAL
- credits_purchased: INT
- bonus_credits: INT
```

### Stripe Products to Create

1. **Credit Packs (One-time Payments)**
   ```
   Product: "Soonic AI Credits - Starter"
   Price: $9.90 one-time
   Metadata: { credits: 5, bonus: 0 }

   Product: "Soonic AI Credits - Standard"
   Price: $24.90 one-time
   Metadata: { credits: 15, bonus: 2 }

   Product: "Soonic AI Credits - Pro"
   Price: $49.90 one-time
   Metadata: { credits: 35, bonus: 5 }
   ```

2. **Subscriptions**
   ```
   Product: "Soonic AI Pro - Monthly"
   Price: $19.90/month recurring

   Product: "Soonic AI Pro - Yearly"
   Price: $149/year recurring
   ```

3. **Lifetime (Launch Special)**
   ```
   Product: "Soonic AI - Lifetime Access"
   Price: $399 one-time
   Metadata: { lifetime: true, launch_special: true }
   ```

### Backend Implementation

1. **Credit Purchase Flow**
   - User clicks "Buy Credits"
   - Create Stripe checkout session (mode: 'payment')
   - On webhook `checkout.session.completed`:
     - Add credits to `profiles.credits_remaining`
     - Store purchase in `credit_purchases` table
     - Set `plan = 'credits'`

2. **Subscription Flow**
   - User clicks "Subscribe"
   - Create Stripe checkout session (mode: 'subscription')
   - On webhook `customer.subscription.created`:
     - Set `plan = 'pro'`
     - Set `analyses_limit = NULL` (unlimited)
     - Store subscription ID

3. **Usage Deduction**
   - On analysis completion:
     - If `plan = 'free'`: increment `analyses_used`
     - If `plan = 'credits'`: decrement `credits_remaining`
     - If `plan = 'pro'`: no limit

### Frontend Implementation

1. **Pricing Page** (`/pricing`)
   - Tab component (Credits, Monthly, Yearly, Lifetime)
   - Pricing cards with CTAs
   - FAQ section

2. **Paywall Modal**
   - Triggered when free limit reached
   - Show usage stats
   - Recommend Standard Pack (highlight)

3. **Dashboard Usage Widget**
   - Show credits remaining or subscription status
   - Progress bar for free users
   - "Buy More Credits" CTA

## 💡 Strategic Insights

### Why NOT Weekly Subscriptions?
- ❌ Too frequent billing (user fatigue)
- ❌ Feels "cheap" (devalues product)
- ❌ High churn (easy to cancel)
- ❌ Administrative overhead (more failed payments)

### Why Credits > Pure Subscription for Your Market?
1. **Church musicians don't analyze daily** (sporadic usage)
2. **Budget-conscious audience** ($24.90 one-time < $19.90/month commitment)
3. **Control over spending** ("I bought 15 credits" vs "another monthly bill")
4. **Higher revenue per transaction** ($24.90 avg vs $12/month)

### Pricing Psychology
- **Anchoring:** $149/year makes $19.90/month seem cheap
- **Scarcity:** "37/50 lifetime spots sold" creates urgency
- **Social Proof:** "Most Popular" badge on Standard Pack
- **Loss Aversion:** "Credits never expire" reduces purchase anxiety

## 🚨 Don't Make These Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake 1: Too Many Tiers
Keep it simple: Free, 3 credit packs, 2 subscriptions, 1 lifetime
- More tiers = decision paralysis

### ❌ Mistake 2: Pricing Too Low
Don't undervalue your product. $12/month is too cheap for this value.
- Your time savings = 90 minutes per song = worth way more

### ❌ Mistake 3: Lifetime as Permanent Offering
Lifetime kills recurring revenue. Use only as launch tactic.
- Cap at 50-100 customers
- Remove after Month 2

### ❌ Mistake 4: Forgetting to Highlight Value
Always show:
- "Save 90 minutes per song"
- "Learn chords 10x faster"
- "Perfect for Sunday worship prep"
